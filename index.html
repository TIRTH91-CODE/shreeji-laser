Our Story

Crafting Precision Since Day One

Shreeji Laser is a precision sheet metal fabrication company headquartered at Shubham Industrial Park, Karvad, Vapi. We deliver end-to-end manufacturing solutions — from CNC laser cutting and bending to welding, fabrication, and powder coating — all under one roof.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced CNC machinery operated by skilled engineers and technicians. We serve a wide range of industries including automotive, textile, pharmaceutical, heavy engineering, and nano engineering sectors.

With a commitment to quality, precision, and on-time delivery, Shreeji Laser has earned the trust of leading brands across India.

Our Services & Capabilities

End-to-end precision metal fabrication — cutting, bending, welding, and coating, all in one place.

Core Capability #1

CNC Laser Cutting

Our high-precision CNC laser cutting machines deliver clean, burr-free cuts on mild steel, stainless steel, and aluminium sheets of varying thicknesses. Ideal for intricate shapes, complex patterns, and tight-tolerance components with exceptional repeatability.

  • ✓ Cutting thickness: MS up to 20 mm, SS up to 16 mm, Aluminium up to 12 mm
  • ✓ Tolerance: ±0.1 mm precision on all cuts
  • ✓ Complex geometries, holes, slots, and profiles
Core Capability #2

CNC Metal Bending

Our CNC press brake machines offer advanced bending capabilities for versatile, high-quality metal forming across a wide range of materials and thicknesses. Precise angle control with fully programmable tooling ensures consistent, repeatable bends.

  • ✓ Material compatibility: MS, SS, GI, aluminium, copper
  • ✓ Bending angles from 0° to 180° with angular accuracy ±0.5°
  • ✓ Complex multi-bend profiles and box sections
Core Capability #3

Welding & Fabrication

Our experienced welding and fabrication team handles complete structural assemblies, industrial components, and custom fabricated parts. We offer MIG, TIG, and arc welding on various metals — delivering strong, clean welds with proper finishing.

  • ✓ MIG, TIG, and arc welding capabilities
  • ✓ Structural fabrication for industrial machinery
  • ✓ Grinding, polishing, and surface preparation included
Core Capability #4

Powder Coating Excellence

Durable, professional-grade powder coating delivering superior corrosion protection and a flawless aesthetic finish — applied with precision using industrial-grade equipment.
